4 atoms, all edges a. The grey face is the base; the amber slice is the right triangle through apex–centroid–corner, with legs x (centroid → corner) and c2 (centroid → apex).
The base is equilateral with sides a. Each spoke from the centroid bisects a 60° corner into two 30° halves, giving the right triangle at each corner. So: x=a3
Apex sits at height c2 above the base centroid; the slant edge to a corner is a. By Pythagoras: x2+(c2)2=a2 Substituting x=a3 gives: ca=83≈1.633
